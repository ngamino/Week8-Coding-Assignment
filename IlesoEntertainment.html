Ileso Entertainment Music & Publishing Corp.

Ileso Entertainment Musit & Publishing Corp. is a label with it's main focus on regional mexican music.

It was founded in August 2019 by Kenneth Santana, President and CEO.

Since it's inception it has published music in the different streaming applications such as: Apple Music, Spotify, Pandora, and Amazon Music to mention a few.
